ABOUT US

Om yoga International, Yoga Ashram located on the banks of Ganga river and surrounded by a beautiful view of Himalayas is an internationally registered Yoga School offering RYS200hour,300hour and 500hour Yoga alliance, USA certified Indian Traditional style ashtanga,Vinyasa and Hatha Yoga Teacher training Courses in Rishikesh, India.
Om Yoga also offers some short term ourses like 8 days Yoga for oldage Training, 30days 200hour yoga for Pregnancy registered from World Yoga Alliance.

WHY CHOOSE OM YOGA

om yoga international is an internationally recognized organization having its main ashram in rishikesh and haridwar, India.It is located on the banks of Ganga river surrounded by the magnificent view of Himalayas. The soothing sound of divine river Ganga and pleasant view of Himalayas makes this place an adequate stay for those who want to do yogic sadhana. The main uniqueness of our Yoga center is that we teach ancient yogic philosophies as taught by our spiritual masters at their time.
